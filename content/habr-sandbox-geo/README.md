# Habr Sandbox GEO Draft

This directory stages a Habr-only rewrite of the blog post
`prikladnoe-geo-optimizaciya-dlya-generativnyh-sistem`.

The draft is intentionally not part of the live Astro blog build. It is an
editorial artifact for author review before submitting to Habr's sandbox.

## Editorial Notes

- The source blog post is short and marketing-oriented. The Habr draft expands
  it into a technical article with definitions, implementation checks,
  examples, measurement notes, and discussion prompts.
- Habr's own sandbox guidance says generated-model articles do not pass
  moderation. Treat the draft as a structured starting point: the named author
  must rewrite it in their own voice, verify claims, and add lived experience
  before submission.
- Keep product mentions minimal. The draft should stand on the usefulness of
  the GEO checklist, not on advertising a company or product.
