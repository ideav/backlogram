# Habr Sandbox GEO Article

This directory stores a Habr-only publication version of the blog post
`prikladnoe-geo-optimizaciya-dlya-generativnyh-sistem`.

The article is intentionally separate from the live Astro blog build. It can be
copied into Habr's editor as a standalone publication package.

## Publication Notes

- The source blog post is short and marketing-oriented. The Habr article expands
  it into a technical article with definitions, implementation checks, examples,
  measurement notes, and discussion prompts.
- The article uses a calmer first-person tone and removes visible editorial
  scaffolding so it reads as a finished Habr submission.
- Keep product mentions minimal. The article should stand on the usefulness of
  the GEO checklist, not on advertising a company or product.
